My favourite recipe is maggie

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of noodles
  • 2 cup of water
  • 1 mini packet of maggie masala

Steps of tasty maggie:

  1. take bowl and 2 cups water
  2. boil water for 2 minutes and then add maggie masala
  3. Add pack of noodles
  4. 2 minutes maggie is ready to eat
